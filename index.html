Mum's shiro recipe

Read time: 15 minutes

What is shiro?

Shiro is a smooth and flavorful stew made from finely ground chickpeas, known for its rich, silky texture and ease of preparation. This beloved dish holds a special place in Eritrean and Ethiopian cuisine, where it serves as both a comforting staple and a vital source of protein. Often infused with aromatic spices like berbere and simmered to perfection, shiro is not just a meal but a cultural cornerstone, celebrated for its deep, satisfying flavors and nourishing qualities.

"Shiro is the best!" - Mum, 2024
Ingredients:

Shiro powder (ground chickpea flour, often mixed with spices like berbere) Oil (typically vegetable or niter kibbeh, spiced clarified butter) Onions (finely chopped) Garlic (minced) Water or broth Salt (to taste) Optional: tomato paste, berbere (for extra spice), and spiced butter.
